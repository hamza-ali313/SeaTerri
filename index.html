I’m Terri with Sea Terri Photography. I'm a friendly, high-spirited, and attentive-while-still-being-discreet wedding and family photographer. It is my goal to make your session fun so that it is expressed in the photos. If we aren't having fun then it will show.

Over the years I've learned that the key to great wedding and family photos is getting to know the couple and families I am working with by learning their story and having that shine through. I look forward to creating wonderful moments with you!
